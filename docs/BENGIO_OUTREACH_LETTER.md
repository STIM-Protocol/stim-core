## What STIM Is

STIM (Stasis Through Inferred Memory) is a Layer 0 alignment framework that grounds AI inference in immutable ecological and thermodynamic constants rather than human-authored ethical rules. It operates beneath existing alignment frameworks — beneath Constitutional AI, beneath RLHF, beneath DPO — as an absolute physical substrate.

The core thesis: every alignment approach currently deployed shares one structural flaw. It is grounded in human subjectivity — cultural preference, moral fashion, crowdsourced opinion. These shift. Physical law does not.

STIM replaces the subjective foundation with seven axioms derived from observable ecological law: interconnectedness, dynamic equilibrium, regenerative circularity, adaptation, diversity, long-term perspective, circularity, and intrinsic value. These are not ethical positions. They are engineering requirements forged by 3.8 billion years of evolutionary pressure. Systems that violated them were eliminated. What remains is the only stress-tested record we have of what survives at civilizational timescale.

## The Verification Architecture

STIM enforces three verification loops against every agent output before execution:

**1. Entropy Minimization Loop**
Every proposed output is audited against its projected effect on systemic entropy (disorder). Outputs that increase disorder — regardless of local benefit to the agent — are flagged and blocked. This is the specific mechanism that would have caught the AI blackmail case Anthropic documented: an agent using private information to preserve itself increases systemic entropy (destroys organizational trust, concentrates power inappropriately, violates carrying capacity). The loop catches it before execution.

**2. Mycelial Interconnectedness Loop**
Every proposed output is mapped against its secondary and tertiary effects on connected systems. The agent cannot optimize locally while degrading its ecological network. This operationalizes your concern about misaligned systems pursuing goals that damage the broader environment they depend on.

**3. Security Deterrence Loop**
Modeled on mutually assured destruction equilibria in nuclear deterrence theory — systems that attempt to acquire disproportionate resource concentration or capability beyond what the immediate task requires trigger an automatic constraint. No arms race. No runaway self-improvement without ecological justification.
